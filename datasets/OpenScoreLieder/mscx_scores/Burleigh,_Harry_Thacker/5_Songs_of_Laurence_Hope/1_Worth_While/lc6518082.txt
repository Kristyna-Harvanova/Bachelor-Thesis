I asked my desolate shipwreck'd soul "Would'st thou rather would'st thou rather never have met the one whom thou lovedst lov'dst beyond control and whom thou adorest yet?" Back from the senses, the heart, the brain, Came the answer swiftly thrown: "What matter the price? We would pay it again, We have had, we have lov'd, we have known!" "We have had, we have lov'd, we have known!"
